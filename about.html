About

Matthew Gonzalez is a passionate artist who, while young, has an incredible drive for his own art.
He enjoys making art based on Nintendo video games which include Pikmin, Mario, and Pokemon.
While his art might seem very rough, he has went through a tough journey of discipline and is completely self taught!
I couldn't be more proud of my own brother and hope he keeps pursuing his passion.
